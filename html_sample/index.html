Chicken Nuggets

Author: Harish
Ingredients
  • Chicken
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Oil
Instructions
  1. Take chicken and cut into pieces
  2. Coat the chicken pieces with bread crumbs
  3. Deep fry the chicken pieces in oil

Pizza

Author: Harish
Ingredients
  • Flour
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
Instructions
  1. Make dough with flour
  2. Spread tomato sauce on the dough
  3. Add cheese on top of the tomato sauce
  4. Bake in pre-heated oven at 300 Celcius for 10 minutes

French Fries

Author: Harish
Ingredients
  • Potato
  • Salt
  • Oil
Instructions
  1. Cut potato into thin slices
  2. Fry the potato slices in oil
  3. Add salt to the fried potato slices
  4. Serve hot
